About Me

In the tail end of my twenties, I am currently based in Brisbane Queensland, Australia. In 2008 I began pursuing a career in the IT development sector, starting in interactive game design and development. Today I am looking for a junior or Graduate position in which I can consolidate my current skills and knowledge, in addition to building and expanding upon my current repertoire of skills. The key to my success is that I am passionate about what I do. I am driven by the pursuit of personal development. I love working on problems and derive satisfaction from a solution well designed and implemented. These traits mean I have a solid work ethic and a no nonsense approach to getting the job done. My long term goal is to find a focused and skilled team, where I can use my knowledge and skills effectively to work on projects I have a passion for, where I will be able to help shape and create effective and efficient solutions to challenging problems.
